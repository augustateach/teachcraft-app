Round 1 · Recognise

Choose the precise phrase

_____ I enjoy boutique hotels, I would not pay twice the usual price for one night.

You need a phrase meaning although I enjoy…

The room was _____ as luxurious as the photographs suggested.

The meaning is: much less luxurious than expected.
Round 2 · Upgrade

Make the answer sound C1+

Basic answer: I prefer expensive hotels because they are cleaner and more comfortable.

Use both: I’d rather … than … not only … but also …
I’d rather stay in a high-end hotel than risk ending up somewhere poorly maintained, as it is likely to be not only more comfortable but also considerably cleaner.
Round 3 · Compare

Two rooms, four moves

Room A
stylish · welcoming · high-end
Room B
run-down · cramped · poorly maintained

Why might people choose these hotels, and how might they feel in the rooms?

At first glance, Room A appears …, whereas Room B … . The first room seems …, which suggests that … . Room B is nowhere near as … as Room A, so guests might …
Round 4 · React

What would you do?

Problem 1

The room is cockroach-ridden.

Problem 2

The toilet will not flush.

Problem 3

The photographs were misleading.

Choose one problem and use: Suppose I discovered that … The first thing I’d do is … What I would do next is …
Round 5 · Repair

Spot the flaw

Which sentence is fully correct?

Repair this sentence:

The second hotel is not nearly comfortable as the first one.
The second hotel is not nearly as comfortable as the first one.
Final challenge

Your one-minute answer

What constitutes a genuinely memorable hotel experience?
